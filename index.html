About Me

I am a PhD Student in the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington .
Previously, I studied Applied Mathematics with a Concentration in Data Science at the University of California, Berkeley.

I am interested in causal inference and its applications in analyzing dependent data (like time series), as well as the applicability of machine learning in the field.

Outside of school and research, I enjoy photography, electronic music, and origami. In Photo Section, you can take a look at some photos I take.
