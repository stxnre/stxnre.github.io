About Me

I am a PhD Student in the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington .
Previously, I studied Applied Mathematics with a Concentration in Data Science at the University of California, Berkeley.

I currently am interested in machine learning and semiparametric methods, in particular for modeling complex time series.

Outside of school and research, I enjoy photography, electronic music, and origami. In Photo Section, you can take a look at some photos I take. At Berkeley, I was also a member (and former Treasurer) of the Cinematic Arts and Production Club (CAP). Movies includes a couple of links to films I was involved in production with.
