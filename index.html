About Me

I am an incoming PhD Student in the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington .
Previously, I studied Applied Mathematics with a Concentration in Data Science at the University of California, Berkeley.

Currently, I am interested in machine learning and its specific use towards sequenced datasets in real-world applications. I became familiar with the topic through natural language processing, but my interest has evolved to include various kinds of signals and time series. I am working towards a career in industry, so I hope to pursue research and professional opportunities that involve this subject.

Outside of school and research, I enjoy photography, electronic music, and origami.
